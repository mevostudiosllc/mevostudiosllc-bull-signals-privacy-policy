Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Mevo Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Bull Signals mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Bull Signals is designed to respect your privacy. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Device token: When you enable push notifications, Apple provides a device token that we store on our server to deliver price alerts. This token cannot identify you personally.
  • Watchlist data: The stocks and cryptocurrencies you track, along with your alert thresholds, are synced to our server solely to monitor prices and send push notifications when your alerts trigger.
  • No account required: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your watchlist, alert settings, groups, and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

Bull Signals uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google): To deliver push notifications. Firebase may collect device identifiers for message delivery. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and interaction data for ad personalization. See Google Privacy Policy. Subscribers do not see ads and no ad-related data is collected for them.
  • Yahoo Finance & CoinGecko: To fetch stock and cryptocurrency prices. These services receive the symbols you track but no personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver price alerts via push notifications
  • To display relevant advertisements (free users only)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Your watchlist data is retained on our server only while you actively use the app. If you disable notifications or uninstall the app, your device registration is removed from our server.

Children's Privacy

Bull Signals is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can disable push notifications at any time in your device Settings.
  • You can subscribe to remove all advertisements.
  • You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
