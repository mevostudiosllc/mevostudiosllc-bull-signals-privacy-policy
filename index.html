Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Mevo Studios LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Bull Signals mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our app.

Your Consent

By downloading, installing, or using the Bull Signals app, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

Bull Signals is designed to respect your privacy. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Device token: When you enable push notifications, Apple provides a device token that we store on our server to deliver price alerts. This token cannot identify you personally.
  • Watchlist data: The stocks and cryptocurrencies you track, along with your alert thresholds, are synced to our server solely to monitor prices and send push notifications when your alerts trigger.
  • No account required: We do not require user accounts or registration. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools of our own.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your watchlist, alert settings, groups, and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Data Retention

Your watchlist data is retained on our server only while you actively use the app. If you disable notifications or uninstall the app, your device registration is removed from our server. Local data persists only as long as the app is installed on your device. We have no ability to recover your local data after deletion.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information as described below:

Google AdMob

We display banner and interstitial advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free version of the app. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (including Apple's IDFA/Advertising ID if you grant permission via the tracking prompt)
  • IP address
  • General location (derived from IP address)
  • App usage and ad interaction data

If you allow tracking, AdMob uses this data to serve more relevant ads. If you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads only. Subscribers do not see ads and no ad-related data is collected for them.

AdMob also uses Apple's SKAdNetwork framework for ad attribution purposes. SKAdNetwork provides aggregated, non-personally-identifiable conversion data to advertising networks without revealing any user-level or device-level information.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

The app includes the Google User Messaging Platform SDK, which may be used to present consent forms to users in regions that require it (such as the European Economic Area under GDPR). This SDK helps manage ad personalization consent preferences. For more information, see Google's UMP documentation.

Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)

The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications for price alerts. Firebase may collect certain technical data such as device type, operating system version, and app instance identifiers. This data is used solely for message delivery and app functionality, and is not used for advertising or tracking purposes. For more information, see Firebase's Privacy Documentation.

Financial Data APIs

The app fetches live price data from:

  • Yahoo Finance API — for stock prices
  • CoinGecko API — for cryptocurrency prices

These requests contain only the stock symbols or coin identifiers you have added. No personal information is sent to these services.

Yahoo Finance RSS (News)

The app fetches news headlines from Yahoo Finance RSS feeds. When you view news for a symbol, the app requests the RSS feed for that ticker. No personal information is sent with these requests.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an auto-renewable monthly subscription ($3.99/month) to remove all advertisements and unlock additional features. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to any payment or billing information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver price alerts via push notifications
  • To display advertisements (free users only)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Ad Tracking

When you first open the app, iOS will ask whether you allow Bull Signals to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. This permission (App Tracking Transparency) controls whether Google AdMob can access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads.

  • If you allow tracking, AdMob may use your IDFA along with other signals to serve more relevant, personalized advertisements.
  • If you deny tracking, ads will still appear but will not be personalized to you.

You can change this choice at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Subscribers are never shown this prompt, as no ads are displayed to subscribers.

Do Not Track

Bull Signals does not independently track users across third-party websites or services. We honor Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. If you have disabled tracking on your device via Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, third-party ad services such as AdMob will serve only non-personalized ads.

Your Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information.

We do not sell personal information. The only data we store on our server is your device token and watchlist/alert configuration, which cannot identify you personally. To remove all data, disable notifications and uninstall the app. To opt out of personalized advertising by AdMob, adjust your device tracking settings as described above.

European Economic Area Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to processing. The data we store (device token and watchlist) is not personally identifiable. You may delete all app data by uninstalling the app, and you may control ad personalization through your device's privacy settings. The Google User Messaging Platform may present a consent form to manage your ad preferences where required by law.

International Users

Bull Signals is available worldwide. By using the app, you understand that certain third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase, CoinGecko, Yahoo Finance) may process data in countries other than your own. These services maintain their own privacy policies governing the transfer and processing of data.

Children's Privacy

Bull Signals is not directed at anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

  • You can disable push notifications at any time in your device Settings.
  • You can allow or deny ad tracking in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • You can subscribe to remove all advertisements.
  • You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data and server-side registrations.
